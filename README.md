# Power BI Economic Data Business Insights

This project presents a Power BI business analysis case study focused on Romanian companies, economic activity, turnover, workforce dynamics, and technological classification between 2016 and 2022.

## Project Overview

The dashboard analyzes economic and workforce indicators across Romanian counties, regions, economic sectors, and technological levels. The main objective is to identify differences in revenue evolution, employee distribution, and business activity patterns between 2016 and 2022.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data modeling
- Data visualization
- Business intelligence reporting

## Key Insights

- Revenue comparison between 2016 and 2022
- Workforce evolution by county and economic sector
- Revenue distribution by technological level and region
- Average revenue by economic subsector
- Employee distribution changes across economic sectors
- Regional differences in business activity and performance
